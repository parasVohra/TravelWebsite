THE DECCAN ODYSSY LUXURY TRAIN
Guest Cabins
Recreating the aesthetics of the erstwhile Deccan dynasties, the 12 guest carriages of the Deccan Odyssey train are categorized as 10 Suite coaches (40 cabins) and 2 Presidential Suite coaches (4 cabins). Each guest carriage is named after an erstwhile ruling dynasty of the Deccan India and reverberates with their architectural aesthetics.
Resturents
The Deccan Odyssey train has two multi cuisine restaurants- Peshwa I and Peshwa II. Each of these two restaurants serves an array of Indian, Continental and Oriental cuisines. Stewards dressed in Maharastrian attire serve food on the platter of the guests.
Conference Car
Offering a seating capacity of 30 people, the conference car is equipped with a range of modern amenities to serve your corporate endeavors
Health Spa
The fitness suite on board Deccan Odyssey train allows guests to follow their workout regime. The facility of steam bath and Ayuvredic body massage will rejuvenate your mind, body and soul.
